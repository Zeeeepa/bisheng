Major Aerospace Historical Events

China's Manned Space Program: Three-Step Strategy

Date: 1992-09-21
Official launch of China's manned space program, establishing the "three-step" development strategy.

Source

Shenzhou 5 Manned Spacecraft Launch

Date: 2003-10-15
China's first manned spacecraft, Shenzhou 5, successfully launched. Astronaut Yang Liwei became the first Chinese in space.

Source

Shenzhou 7: First Spacewalk

Date: 2008-09-25
China's astronauts performed their first spacewalk, breaking new ground in extravehicular activity technology.

Tianhe Core Module Launch

Date: 2021-04-29
Launch of the Tianhe core module, marking the start of China's space station assembly.

Cutting-edge Aerospace Technologies

X-59 Quiet Supersonic Technology (Quesst)

Special airframe design and sonic boom management reduce noise to a gentle "thump" at ground level.
Application: Future commercial supersonic jets.
Source: NASA

Advanced Air Mobility (AAM)

Electric propulsion, vertical takeoff/landing, and 5G enable smart, safe urban air transport.
Application: Air taxis, cargo drones, emergency rescue.
Source: NASA

Nancy Grace Roman Space Telescope

Wide-field infrared imaging, 200x larger than Hubble's IR camera.
Application: Dark energy research, exoplanet detection.
Source: NASA

Space Communications & Navigation (SCaN)

Next-gen deep space communication (laser, auto-navigation).
Application: Artemis lunar missions, Mars exploration.
Source: NASA

SWOT Satellite

Radar interferometry for precise water surface measurement.
Application: Ocean, lake, river monitoring, disaster warning.
Source: NASA

IXPE X-ray Polarimetry Explorer

Measures X-ray polarization to reveal black holes and pulsars.
Application: Astrophysics research.
Source: NASA
